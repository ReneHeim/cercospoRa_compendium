This dataset contains:

- Multispectral imagery obtained from an uncrewed aerial system (UAS). Acquired over our experimental site in Göttingen, Germany, in July 2022.
- Multispectral Sentinel-2 imagery (https://dataspace.copernicus.eu) acquired over our experimental site in Göttingen, Germany, in July 2022.
- Super-resolved Sentinel-2 imagery.
- Shape files as geopackage (.gpkg) to extract multispectral data for the regions of interest.

Copernicus Sentinel data are made available under the European Union’s open data policy.
For more information, see: https://www.copernicus.eu/en/about-copernicus/copernicus-data-policy
